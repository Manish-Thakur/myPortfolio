5 STEPS TO BEAT PROCRASTINATION
Published on | December 15, 2018

Some people spend so much time procrastinating that they are unable to complete important daily tasks. They may have a strong desire to stop procrastinating but feel they cannot do so. How do I stop being lazy and procrastinating?. Don't worry, here I'm going to share with you how to avoid procrastination and laziness.

Read More...

1. VISION: Our today's world has many things to offer and choices are abundant to choose from. Therefore, having a clear vision of what you wanna be is important. "How can you decide on your vision"?. This answer comes from self introspection of where do you see yourself 5 years from now or what personality you wanna built over the years ahead. Self-Assessment helps you identify your strengths and weakness and helps you choose the right vision and profession.


2. CLARITY ON VISION: Now once you have set your vision. Its time to choose the right path that will help you achieve that vision. A vision without clarity can be confusing, boring and unrealistic. So, before you start on your journey, it gets important to keep your head clear and focus should be only on achieving goals.


3. SET SMART GOALS: While deciding your vision,it is also important to set goals that will help you in achieving it. Your goals should be Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Realistic and Timely. You can further breakdown your approach by setting Short-Term and Long-Term goals. A Short-Term goal can be a six month goal whereas a Long-Term goal can last a year or beyond. No matter how you achieve your goals; it should be SMART.


4. ACTION PLAN: SMART goals set with proper action plan go handy. Now that you have Purpose and Path, all you need is the Will to implement it. You need to have absolute faith on yourself and do not get carried away by emotions of failures, heartbreaks and stress. Create a plan that will help you balance your aspirational and personal life and have a disciplined life.


5. PROGRESS CHECKER: With work you also need to setup a mechanism to monitor your progress. This timely check-ins helps you to analyse the difference and take proper actions.
